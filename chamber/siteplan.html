WDD 231 Site Plan

Site Name

NaijaBizConnect

This name highlights the mission of connecting local businesses and professionals in Nigeria. It's memorable, relevant to the location and audience, and gives insight into the site’s purpose.

Optional domain: naijabizconnect.org

Site Purpose

The site will provide resources for local Nigerian entrepreneurs and businesses, including membership services, promotional opportunities, a directory, weather information, and community event listings.

Scenarios

  • How can I register my business to be listed in the directory?
  • What are the membership levels and benefits available for my organization?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #005fa3 – used for headers, buttons, and highlights
  • Accent Color: #f4a261 – used for call-to-action areas, icons, and secondary highlights
  • Neutral background: #f9f9f9

Typography

  • Poppins – used for all body text and headings for a clean, modern look
  • Backup fonts: sans-serif

Wireframe

Mobile Layout (small screen)

  • Header with logo and nav toggle
  • Main content stacked: intro, weather, events, membership form
  • Cards stack vertically
  • Footer with contact and social links

Desktop Layout (larger screen)

  • Header with full nav bar
  • Two-column layout:
    • Main form on the left
    • Membership cards on the right
  • Footer remains at bottom with full width
